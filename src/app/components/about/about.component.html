A Minimal Team For a Better World

HandMade is the first platform for buying and selling hand crafts. We create a link between the product of your dreams found the best quality and the most appropriate price.Handmade is used for something that is made by hand only, without any usage of machines. On the other hand, custom-made we use when something is made especially for somebody – by its measures/wishes or for special occasion. What is at the same time handmade and custom-made, such is, for example, clothing, in most cases has very high price.

our mission is to connect people to handmade home decor that is beautiful, high quality, functional, and simple. They want to help people decorate their homes with rustic decor that will help friends and families connect and enjoy each other’s company.
